About Abel

Name: Abel Jose

Date of Birth: December 12, 1980

Nationality: Undetermined (Originating from a lineage of explorers)

Address: An antique-filled apartment in an unnamed port city, surrounded by a cluttered library of old maps and navigational texts.
 He can often be found in various remote locations while pursuing his research.

Contact Information: Preferring traditional communication, he is most reliably reached by telegram or a handwritten letter,
 delivered to his home address. He does not use a cell phone.


Educational Qualifications:

1998–2002: Master of Cartography and Historical Geography from the Royal Geographic Society, London.

1994–1998: Bachelor of Arts in History, specializing in ancient civilizations and folklore, from an unspecified European university.


Abel Jose lives a life of quiet intensity,
perpetually in pursuit of the ghosts of the past. 
Driven by a childhood tragedy and an heirloom compass,
he seeks to honor his family's legacy by completing his grandfather's work.
He is a man at odds with a world that values data over dreams and lives by the quote,
"It is impossible to fail if you gave it your all; if you still fail, it means you never gave it your all".
